Welcome From Pyay

The rapid growth of tourism industory in Myanmar, the west district of Bago Division, Pyay attracts many tourists because of Sri Kestra, Pyu Ancient Cities.

It was recognized as a World Heritage on 22.6.2014. Today the city is becoming one of the tourist destinations in Myanmar.
